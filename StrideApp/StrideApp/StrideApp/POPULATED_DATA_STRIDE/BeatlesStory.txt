The Beatles Story is a museum in Liverpool about the Beatles and their history. It is located on the historical Royal Albert dock The Beatles Story was opened on the 1st of May 1990. The Beatles Story contains Recreations of the The Casbah Coffee Club, The Cavern Club club and Abbey Road Studios among all the historical Beatles items such as John Lennon spectacles, George Harrison's first guitar and the detailed history about the British Invasion on the solo careers of every people in the exhibition have been visited by over four million people from over a hundred countries by 2015. The museum was also recognized as one of the best tourist attractions of the United Kingdom in 2015. The exhibition was preceded by the Cavern Mecca and Beatle City.
